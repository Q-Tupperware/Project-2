K2-18: An Ocean Planet

K2-18 is a Super Earth 8.92 times Earth's mass which orbits it's star every 32.9 days which is believed to be covered in oceans of liquid water. This fact, along with it being in the habitable zone and the detection of methane and carbon dioxide in it's hydrogen-rich atmosphere, make it a stong contender for the search for extraterrestrial life, though nothing is certain.
